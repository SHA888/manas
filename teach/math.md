# Manas — Basic Mathematics Knowledge

## Addition

Addition combines numbers.

The plus sign means addition.

For the question what is addition, addition combines numbers.

For the question what is 2 + 2, the answer is 4.

2 + 2 = 4.

5 + 3 = 8.

10 + 15 = 25.

Adding zero to a number keeps the number the same.

1 + 0 = 1.

0 + 7 = 7.

2 + 3 = 5.

4 + 4 = 8.

6 + 7 = 13.

20 + 30 = 50.

## Subtraction

Subtraction removes one number from another.

The minus sign means subtraction.

For the question what is subtraction, subtraction removes one number from another.

5 - 2 = 3.

10 - 4 = 6.

20 - 5 = 15.

Subtracting zero from a number keeps the number the same.

7 - 0 = 7.

9 - 3 = 6.

100 - 50 = 50.

## Multiplication

Multiplication is repeated addition.

The multiplication sign can be written as `x`.

For the question what is multiplication, multiplication is repeated addition.

3 x 4 = 12.

5 x 5 = 25.

10 x 10 = 100.

Multiplying by one keeps the number the same.

9 x 1 = 9.

Multiplying by zero gives zero.

9 x 0 = 0.

2 x 6 = 12.

4 x 8 = 32.

## Division

Division splits a number into equal parts.

The division sign can be written as `/`.

For the question what is division, division splits a number into equal parts.

10 / 2 = 5.

12 / 3 = 4.

100 / 10 = 10.

Dividing a number by one keeps the number the same.

8 / 1 = 8.

20 / 5 = 4.

9 / 3 = 3.

## Fractions

A fraction represents part of a whole.

For the question what is a fraction, a fraction represents part of a whole.

One half is written as 1/2.

One quarter is written as 1/4.

Two halves make one whole.

Four quarters make one whole.

The top number of a fraction is called the numerator.

The bottom number of a fraction is called the denominator.

1/2 is greater than 1/4.

## Decimals

A decimal can represent part of a whole.

Decimals use a decimal point.

For the question what is a decimal, a decimal can represent part of a whole.

0.5 is equal to one half.

0.25 is equal to one quarter.

1.0 is equal to one whole.

2.5 means two and one half.

## Percentages

A percentage is a number out of 100.

For the question what is a percentage, a percentage is a number out of 100.

50 percent means 50 out of 100.

50 percent is equal to one half.

25 percent is equal to one quarter.

100 percent means the whole amount.

10 percent means 10 out of 100.

For the question what is an even number, an even number can be divided by 2 with no remainder.

## Comparison

Greater than means larger.

Less than means smaller.

Equal means the same value.

10 is greater than 5.

3 is less than 7.

4 is equal to 4.

For the question what does greater than mean, greater than means larger.

For the question what does less than mean, less than means smaller.

## Geometry

Geometry studies shapes.

A triangle is a shape with 3 sides.

For the question what is a triangle, a triangle is a shape with 3 sides.

A square has 4 equal sides.

A rectangle has 4 sides.

A circle has no corners.

A corner is a point where two sides meet.

A line is a straight path.

An angle is formed when two lines meet.

For the question what is a square, a square has 4 equal sides.

For the question what is a circle, a circle has no corners.

## Area and Perimeter

Area measures the space inside a shape.

Perimeter measures the distance around a shape.

For the question what is area, area measures the space inside a shape.

For the question what is perimeter, perimeter measures the distance around a shape.

The area of a rectangle is width times height.

The perimeter of a square is the sum of its 4 sides.

If a square has side length 5, its perimeter is 20.

If a rectangle has width 4 and height 3, its area is 12.

## Order of Operations

Order of operations tells which math steps happen first.

Parentheses are solved first.

Multiplication and division are usually done before addition and subtraction.

Addition and subtraction are usually done after multiplication and division.

In `2 + 3 x 4`, multiplication happens before addition.

The value of `2 + 3 x 4` is 14.

For the question what is order of operations, order of operations tells which math steps happen first.

## Place Value

Place value tells the value of a digit based on its position.

In the number 42, the digit 4 means four tens.

In the number 42, the digit 2 means two ones.

In the number 100, the digit 1 means one hundred.

For the question what is place value, place value tells the value of a digit based on its position.
